Tumeric Tahini Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger (paste, not powdered)
  • 2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1-2 tsp ground cumin
  • water (to preferred thickness)

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Put all ingredients in Bonne Maman jelly jar
  3. Add water until jar is 80% full
  4. Shake vigorously until combined
  5. Check texture and taste, add more water or seasoning if needed
